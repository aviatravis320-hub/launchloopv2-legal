Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: February 1, 2026

You have the right to delete your LaunchLoop account and associated personal data at any time. This page explains exactly how, what gets removed, and what (if anything) we keep for legal compliance.

Option 1 — Delete From Inside the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the LaunchLoop app:

1
Open the LaunchLoop app and sign in to your account.
2
Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap “Delete Account”.
4
Read the confirmation dialog carefully, then tap “Confirm Delete”.
5
You will be signed out immediately. Your account and personal data will be permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot sign in, email us:

To: aviatravis320@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include:

  • The email address associated with your LaunchLoop account
  • Your username (if you remember it)
  • A short statement: "I request deletion of my LaunchLoop account and personal data."

We will verify your identity (to prevent unauthorized deletions) and complete deletion within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once complete.

Things to Know Before You Delete

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Any unused credits in your wallet will be forfeited.
  • Your submitted apps will be removed from the LaunchLoop feed.
  • If you sign up again later with the same email, you will start with a brand-new account.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or your data, email aviatravis320@gmail.com. We typically respond within 48 hours.
